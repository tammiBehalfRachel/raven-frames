What we do

Every frame is crafted to preserve your artwork in its best aspect. During the consultation process, we will consider the colours, textures, finishes, and proportions of the mounting and framing, and advise on the best level of consultation for the project.

Each artwork is treated with the care and attention it deserves. We use conservation or museum grade materials and use only reversible methods, so that no part of the framing process alters the work. Our frames are made from either solid wood (usually Forest Stewardship Council certified) or aluminium. We avoid using plastic in the construction and packaging of frames wherever possible.

Examples of our (framing) services include:

  • float and window mounting
  • embroidery and tapestry stretching
  • garment framing
  • canvas tray framing
  • hand-painting/finishing and colour matching
  • UV protective and anti-reflective glazing
  • mirror glazing
